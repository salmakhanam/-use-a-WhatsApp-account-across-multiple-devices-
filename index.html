WhatsApp's multi-device feature enables users to access their WhatsApp account from up to four different devices simultaneously, without the need to constantly switch between devices or log in and out repeatedly. This feature is particularly useful for individuals who rely on multiple devices throughout their day, such as smartphones, tablets, desktop computers, and even web browsers.
